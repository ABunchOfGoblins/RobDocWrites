Former NYC Midnight Submissions
NYC Midnight is an organization that puts on multiple creative writing challenges throughout the year under tight deadlines.
Found below are various stories I've submitted accordingly and, when relevant, I've noted the prompts and length requirements I may have had to work with.
"Sfumato" -- A young artist, growing disillusioned from his work as he grows more popular, disconnects himself to gain perspective.
"Spilled Ink" -- Two sailors encounter a horror at sea (flash fiction, 100 words).
